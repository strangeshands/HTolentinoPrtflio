My PCK Reflection

After taking the PCK test, I was honestly surprised at how accurate and informative the results were. Seeing Conventional and Enterprising at the top made sense, but I still feel that the Investigative side represents me just as much, especially with how I enjoy problem-solving and programming. The suggested careers, like Compliance Manager and Computer Network Support Specialist, actually fit the kind of structured and responsible work I see myself doing. What I realized through this is that these results can guide me as I move closer to graduation, but they shouldn’t box me in. I should commit to improving the strengths I already have, like working systematically (which I even practice now in CSSWENG & STSWENG), while staying open to change since my interests and goals can still evolve. In the end, this whole experience helped me understand myself better and reminded me that while I want a meaningful career, at the bare minimum, I just want a job that can support me and help me survive.
